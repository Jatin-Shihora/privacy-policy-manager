## Introduction

Welcome to Quick Math Daily ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application Quick Math Daily (the "App") handles information when you use our services.

Quick Math Daily is designed to help you improve your mental math skills through daily practice with adaptive difficulty. We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly what data our App accesses and why.

---

## Information We DO NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we **do not** do:

- **No Personal Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.
- **No User Accounts:** The App does not require registration or login. There are no user accounts.
- **No Cloud Storage:** Your game progress, statistics, achievements, and preferences are stored locally on your device only.
- **No Data Selling:** We never sell, trade, or rent any user data to third parties.
- **No Third-Party Analytics:** We do not use analytics services to track your behavior outside of standard ad network functionality.

---

## Information the App Stores Locally

To provide its functionality, Quick Math Daily stores the following data locally on your device:

### 1. Game Progress Data
- **What:** Problems attempted, correct answers, accuracy statistics, current level, XP earned
- **Why:** To track your progress and provide personalized difficulty adjustment
- **Storage:** Local Room database on your device only

### 2. Streak Data
- **What:** Current streak count, longest streak, daily play status
- **Why:** To maintain your daily practice streak and provide motivation
- **Storage:** Local Room database on your device only

### 3. Achievement Data
- **What:** Unlocked achievements, progress toward achievements
- **Why:** To reward your accomplishments and track milestones
- **Storage:** Local Room database on your device only

### 4. User Preferences
- **What:** Sound/vibration settings, notification preferences, theme preference (dark/light mode)
- **Why:** To remember your app settings
- **Storage:** Local DataStore on your device only

### 5. Difficulty Settings
- **What:** Your selected learning path, current difficulty level, adaptive difficulty metrics
- **Why:** To provide appropriately challenging problems based on your skill level
- **Storage:** Local Room database on your device only

---

## How the App Works

1. You solve math problems at an adaptive difficulty level
2. Your answers and response times are analyzed locally to adjust difficulty
3. Progress, streaks, and achievements are saved to local storage
4. **No data ever leaves your device** - everything happens locally (except for advertisement loading)

## Advertising

Quick Math Daily is a free, ad-supported application. We use Google AdMob to display advertisements.

### Ad Types Used
- **Banner Ads:** Small rectangular ads shown at the bottom of screens (Home, Game)
- **Interstitial Ads:** Full-screen ads shown between game sessions (with time and problem-count restrictions)
- **Rewarded Video Ads:** Optional ads you can watch to earn hint refills

### Ad Timing (User-Friendly Approach)
- Minimum 90 seconds between interstitial ads
- Minimum 5 problems completed between ads
- Ads are not shown during negative emotional states
- Ads are not shown near milestone achievements

### What AdMob May Collect
- Device advertising identifier (Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Device information (model, operating system version)
- Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

### More Information
For details about how Google AdMob handles data and your choices regarding ad personalization, please review:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [How Google Uses Data](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)
- [Google Advertising Policies](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

## Notifications

If you enable notifications, the App may send:

### Daily Reminders
- Time: Configurable (default 9:00 AM)
- Purpose: Remind you to practice and maintain your streak
- Content: Motivational messages encouraging practice

### Streak Warnings
- Time: Evening (8:00 PM)
- Purpose: Alert you if your streak is at risk
- Content: Reminder to play before midnight to maintain streak

**You can disable notifications at any time** through the App settings or your device's notification settings.

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
All your data is stored locally on your device:
- Game statistics and progress
- Achievements and milestones
- Streak data
- App preferences and settings

### No Cloud Sync
We do not offer cloud synchronization. Your data never leaves your device unless:
- The App loads advertisements (which requires internet)

### Data Deletion
Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Uninstalling the App removes all App-related data
- You can clear App data anytime via Android Settings > Apps > Quick Math Daily > Clear Data
- Your progress cannot be recovered once deleted

- We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13

## Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:
