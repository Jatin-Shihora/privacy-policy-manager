## Introduction

Welcome to Cosmic: Music Player ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application Cosmic: Music Player (the "App") handles information when you use our services.

Cosmic: Music Player is designed as a local, offline music player for Android devices. We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly what data our App accesses and why.

---

## Information We DO NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we **do not** do:

- **No Personal Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.
- **No User Accounts:** The App does not require registration or login. There are no user accounts.
- **No Analytics or Tracking:** We do not use analytics services, tracking pixels, or any form of user behavior tracking.
- **No Advertising:** The App contains no advertisements and does not share data with advertising networks.
- **No Data Selling:** We never sell, trade, or rent any user data to third parties.
- **No Cloud Storage:** Your music library, playlists, and preferences are stored locally on your device only.

---

## Information the App Accesses

To provide its functionality, Cosmic: Music Player requires access to certain device features and data. All data accessed remains on your device unless you explicitly use features that require internet connectivity.

### 1. Audio Files (Required)
- **What:** Access to audio files stored on your device
- **Why:** To scan, organize, and play your music library
- **Permission:** `READ_MEDIA_AUDIO`
- **Storage:** Locally on your device only

### 2. Images and Album Art (Required)
- **What:** Access to images on your device
- **Why:** To display album artwork and artist images embedded in your music files
- **Permission:** `READ_MEDIA_IMAGES`
- **Storage:** Locally on your device only

### 3. Storage Access (For Downloads)
- **What:** Read and write access to device storage
- **Why:** To save downloaded songs, export playlists, and manage music files
- **Permission:** `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`, `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`
- **Storage:** Locally on your device only

### 4. Internet Access (Optional Features)
- **What:** Network connectivity
- **Why:** Required only for optional features:
  - Fetching song lyrics from online sources
  - Downloading songs via online URL import feature
  - Downloading artist images
- **Permission:** `INTERNET`, `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`
- **Note:** The App works fully offline. Internet is only used when you explicitly request online features.

## Third-Party Services

When you use certain optional features, the App may communicate with the following third-party services:

### 1. Lyrics Service (Lrclib)
- **When Used:** When you request synced lyrics for a song
- **Data Sent:** Song title, artist name, album name, and song duration
- **Purpose:** To find and display matching lyrics
- **Privacy:** We send only the minimum information needed to match lyrics. No personal data is transmitted.

### 2. Artist Images (Last.fm/MusicBrainz)
- **When Used:** When auto-downloading artist images is enabled
- **Data Sent:** Artist name
- **Purpose:** To fetch and display artist photographs
- **Privacy:** Only artist names are transmitted. No personal data is collected.

**Note:** These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:
- Lrclib: Open-source lyrics database

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
All your data is stored locally on your device:
- Music library metadata
- Playlists and favorites
- App settings and preferences
- Downloaded songs and lyrics
- Equalizer settings

### No Cloud Sync
We do not offer cloud synchronization. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly:
- Share a song using Android's share feature
- Export a playlist file
- Use internet-dependent features (lyrics, downloads)

### Data Deletion
Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Uninstalling the App removes all App-related data
- You can clear App data anytime via Android Settings
- Downloaded music files remain in your device storage until you delete them

## Children's Privacy

Cosmic: Music Player is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since our App does not collect personal information from any users, this applies to all age groups equally.

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Purpose | Required? |
|------------|---------|-----------|
| `READ_MEDIA_AUDIO` | Access your music files | Yes |
| `READ_MEDIA_IMAGES` | Display album artwork | Yes |
| `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` | Access music on older Android versions | Yes (Android 12 and below) |
| `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` | Save downloads, edit tags | Optional features |
| `INTERNET` | Lyrics, downloads, artist images | Optional features |
| `WAKE_LOCK` | Keep music playing when screen is off | Yes |
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` | Background music playback | Yes |
| `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` | Restore widgets after device restart | Optional |

## Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

1. **Deny Permissions:** You can deny any permission, though core features require audio access.
2. **Disable Internet Features:** Use the App completely offline by disabling internet access for the App in Android Settings.
3. **Clear Data:** Clear all App data anytime via Android Settings > Apps > Cosmic: Music Player > Clear Data.
4. **Uninstall:** Remove the App and all associated data at any time.
5. **Blacklist Folders:** Hide specific folders from your music library within the App.
